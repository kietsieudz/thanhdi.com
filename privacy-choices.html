User Privacy Choices

Updated: — Options for data & account control.

Summary

You can request account deletion and personal data removal for the TDStudio Game. We verify requests to protect your account, and we aim to complete processing within 30 days.

How to Submit a Request

  • Email: send your request to contact@thanhdi.top (use the subject “Privacy Request”).
  • Support Form: thanhdi.top/contact.html (choose the Privacy topic).
  • Include: your username and a brief description (e.g., “delete account” or “remove gameplay data”).

What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request and may ask for additional verification.
  • Local data is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Server-side data is deleted after we complete verification and confirm the action with you.
